OCT 1, 2020 by NSW Business Map Team

How can we leverage publicly available data from NSW gov to better inform business decisions?


Project Statement

NSW businesses are struggling from bushfire and COVID effects. Our team is looking at innovative ways to inform aspiring/recovering businesses of less risky opportunities.

We want to use data in the public as well as govt domain to help businesses understand demographic as well as service information around their specific areas.


Expected Outcome

Build a smart tool that visualises aggregated data and recommendations for small businesses and helps them understand where best to open up/scale.


Overview Condition

Performance of the economy:

Business perceptions about the performance NSW economy fell to record lows in June. But there was a strong recovery in businesses expectations for the September quarter suggesting the reopening of the economy and strong support from governments have boosted business optimism.

Business expansion:

Businesses have continued to scale back their capital spending and staffing levels. Around 39 per cent of respondents indicated their staffing levels were lower compared to the previous quarter while 57 per cent indicated capital spending was also down.

Industry assessment:

Arts and recreation and accommodation and food services, have been the most severely impacted reflecting exposure to government restrictions and weak customer demand.

(Source from:businessaustralia.com)

To make NSW the easiest state for small business to start, run, adapt & prosper

August 1, 2020 by NSW Business Map Team

How can we leverage publicly available data from NSW gov to better inform business decisions?


This project will prefer using more technology innovation for small business start or develop their firms. Stodder had a professional experiment with business intelligence pointed that data visualization will bring more effectiveness and reduce time on making decisions for non-technical background business (Stodder,2015). Moreover, some datasets and government reports are not easily accessible resources for them. Data mining will demonstrate more potential information after processing data, the data will provide a variety of information close to their interest and business dashboard will make certain decisions.

Most of the existing solutions only rely on the traditional management measurement, while Bento had research about prediction start-up success with machine learning methods(Bento, 2018). Final result was realized using random forest to predict success based on key metrics about the business. The machine learning model would give insight into business running and give more predictable future suggestions.

This project will use data to help small business recovery drivers that will boost confidence. At the same time, We will drive data mining technology which allows the small business to run closer to its potential. Small businesses have the ability to choose the information of their main concert and this project system gives recommendation or based on their preference gives suggestion. The data visualization, could become more comprehensible for different cultures or educated people, gives more direct visual perception.
